My Work

Almost 15 years ago now I contracted with a web develpment agency and paid the 50% deposit. Things went downhill from there; they effectively sold me a template, and I ultimately kissed goodbye to my money.

Ready to hear a joke? I asked them to change the menu and they said "not possible as it's an image..."
I mean, what!

Since then I've started three businesses marketed solely online. I've done this by wireframing and writing the content, and then having a really good graphic designer put a skin over it.

Then I've used a combination of WP themes, visual editors, and a sprinkling of developers to make the sites a reality.
I always wated to code but just didn't have a way to learn while doing - until now!
