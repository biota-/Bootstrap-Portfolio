My Work

It's almost 15 years now since I paid 50% deposit to a seemingly credible web design studio. Things went downhill from there; they effectively sold me a template, and I eventually kissed goodbye to my money.

As an example, I asked them to change the menu and they said "not possible as it's an image..." (!)

So from there on in I figured it out myself. First experimenting with the (very limited) visual builders of the day, and later setting up a couple of Wordpress themes and content.

Eventually though, something bespoke was necessary. This time I just didn't have the budget to get what I wanted professionally, so it was up to me to find a way!

For the Duranite and Adeptus sites I was able to devise all content and necessary elements, and wireframe the sites around these. Then I used an experienced graphic designer for the 'look and feel', and had the whole thing coded. I am hopeful I will be able to code Adeptus v2.0 myself.

Copywriting has seemed to come naturally to me, although at first, it took me an inordinate amount of time. Even so, I would say that effective copywriting has been my highest ROI activity to date.
